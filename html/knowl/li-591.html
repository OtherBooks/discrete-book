Item c
Disaster Strikes Gridtown: there is a pothole on 4th avenue between 5th and 6th street. How many routes to work can you take avoiding that unsightly (and dangerous) stretch of road? Solution

Routes to work that hit the pothole: \({7 \choose 3}1{14 \choose 8}\).

There for the number of routes to work which avoid the pothole are \begin{equation*} {22 \choose 11} - {7 \choose 3}{14 \choose 8} \end{equation*}
