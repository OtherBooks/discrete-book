Let \(A = \{1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6\}\), \(B = \{2, 4, 6\}\), \(C = \{1, 2, 3\}\) and \(D = \{7, 8, 9\}\). If the universe is \(\U = \{1, 2, \ldots, 10\}\), find:

  1. \(A \cup B\).
  2. \(A \cap B\).
  3. \(B \cap C\).
  4. \(A \cap D\).
  5. \(\bar{B \cup C}\).
  6. \(A \setminus B\).
  7. \((D \cap \bar C) \cup \bar{A \cap B}\).
  8. \(\emptyset \cup C\).
  9. \(\emptyset \cap C\).
