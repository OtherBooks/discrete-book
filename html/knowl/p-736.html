We might replace the \(a\) with another letter, and sometimes we omit \(a_0\), starting with \(a_1\) instead. The numbers in the subscripts are called indices (the plural of index). We can think of the terms in the sequence as the outputs of a function with domain \(\N\): the \(n\)th term in the sequence is \(f(n) = \gls{an}\).
