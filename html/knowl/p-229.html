Returning to our example above, we have \(|A| = 12\), \(|B| = 5\), \(|C| = 8\). We also have \(|A \cap B| = 2\), \(|A \cap C| = 6\), \(|B \cap C| = 3\), and \(|A \cap B \cap C| = 1\). Therefore: \begin{equation*} |A \cup B \cup C| = 12 + 5 + 8 - 2 - 6 - 3 + 1 = 15 \end{equation*}
