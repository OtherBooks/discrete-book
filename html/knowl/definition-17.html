Definition4.1.3

{Recursive definition} A recursive definition (sometimes called an inductive definition) for a sequence \(a_0, a_1, a_2, \ldots\) consists of a recurrence relation: an equation relating a term of the sequence to previous terms (terms with smaller index) and an initial condition: a list of a few terms of the sequence (one less than the number of terms in the recurrence relation).
