Your Blu-ray collection consists of 9 comedies and 7 horror movies. Give an example of a question for which the answer is:

  1. 16.
  2. 63.
Answer
  1. 16 is the number of choices you have if you want to watch one movie, either a comedy or horror flick.
  2. 63 is the number of choices you have if you will watch two movies, first a comedy and then a horror.
